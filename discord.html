Welcome to the Discord Bot

Your all-in-one EVE Online market and utility assistant for Discord.

Add to Your Discord Server

Features

Real-time Market Data

Get live market prices for any item from all major EVE trade hubs, including Jita, Amarr, and Dodixie.

D-Scan Parser

Paste directional scan results to get an instant, categorized breakdown of all ships on scan.

LP Store Analysis

Analyze and compare Loyalty Point store offers to find the most profitable items to cash in your LP.

Commands

Command Description
/market <item> Get current market prices for an item.
/dscan Opens a pop-up form to paste and analyze d-scan results.
/lp <corporation> Browse the LP store for a specific NPC corporation.
/build <item> Calculate the manufacturing cost for a buildable item.
/help Displays a list of all available commands.
